What is Nostr? How to get started

Notes and Other Stuff Transmitted by Relays Nostr is a relay-based network that broadcasts your messages to multiple servers. This design helps to make the network more resistant to censorship and also ensures that the network is distributed, providing a higher level of availability and reliability.

NOSTR Lightning integrating By integrating Bitcoin and Lightning into NOSTR, not only do users unlock a decentralized way to transact funds, but relays are also given a way to monetize or control for denial-of-service (DOS) spam attacks.
